About This Project

Posted on January 10, 2025 by Author Name

The “House Rules” project is a passion-driven endeavor to merge tabletop gaming culture with video game development insights. Our goal is to provide a space for shared rulesets, dev-logs, and creative writing that inspire both players and developers alike.

From behind-the-scenes peeks into our 2D platformer’s development to custom board-game expansions, we aim to bring ideas to life that spark collaboration and creativity.

Our Goals & Vision

Posted on January 12, 2025 by Author Name

  • **Community-Driven:** Encourage feedback, suggestions, and shared ideas.
  • **Open Development:** Transparent dev-logs on game features and design decisions.
  • **Cross-Medium Inspiration:** Bridging the gap between tabletop mechanics and digital gaming experiences.

We believe in iterative growth, listening to our players, and evolving “House Rules” to align with what resonates best. If you have any questions or ideas, feel free to drop a line in our contact section!
